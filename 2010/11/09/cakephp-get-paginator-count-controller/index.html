CakePHP get paginator count in controller

2010-11-09

Pagination in CakePHP is pretty awesome and all, but sometimes things are just too magic, and I end up searching for something that is obviously there but I’m not sure where.

Today it was getting the total matches in the controller - this is easy in the view, it’s just:

$paginator->counter();

After checking the paginator helper source, it seems to be accessing $this->params[‘paging’]. So, in your controller, just do:

$this->params['paging'][$model]['count']

to get the total count.
