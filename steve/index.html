Who are you?

This is the web home of Steve Anderson(me).

I'm a developer and general web geek. I like the web, food, FOSS and Python, among other things.

I was Time's Person of the Year 2006 and 2011, and The Guardian's "MediaGuardian 100 most influential person" 2013.

I have a very particular set of skills. Skills I have aquired over a very long career. Skills that make me a nightmare for people like you.

I've got so much things to say, that I thought I'd start a blog.

I was looking for a job, and then I found a job (in web development), and heaven knows I'm miserable now.

My new hobby is cramming references to popular culture in my blog description.
