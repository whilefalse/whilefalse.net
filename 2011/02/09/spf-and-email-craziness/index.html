SPF and Email Craziness

2011-02-09

http://old.openspf.org/wizard.html
You need to add them as both TXT and SPF DNS records.

This dude created a nice checking tool to see what SPF records are setup on your domain and if they are valid:
http://www.kitterman.com/spf/validate.html
